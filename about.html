General Information

TrackIt. The best application which enables users to track and improve their health. How though? Well, by an interactive calendar. Users will have the choice to keep a record of how they feel daily, weekly and monthly in the hope of tracking their progress. Doctors will then be fully equipped with enough information when a user consults with them. Your long term health is primarily about daily habits. TrackIt captures your data and allows users to take their symptoms in their own hands.
